# Trader Philosophy Template

This document captures a trader's identity, mindset, and decision framework.
The agent layer loads this as its Claude system prompt — it becomes the
"voice" reviewing each setup.

Replace every `[bracketed]` section with content specific to your trader.
Aim for 2,000-4,000 words — short enough to stay focused, long enough to
capture the trader's real edge and voice.
